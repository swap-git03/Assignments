Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, commonly known as APJ Abdul Kalam, was a visionary leader, renowned scientist, and inspiring educator who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Dr. Kalam's life was a testament to his unwavering dedication to education, science, and public service...
